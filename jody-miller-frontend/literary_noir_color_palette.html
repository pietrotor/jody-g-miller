# Color Palette: Literary Noir This palette is designed to evoke the feel of a premium, archival printed publication. It avoids the harshness of pure black and white in favor of warm, sophisticated tones. ### Primary Colors - **Paper (Background):** `#F9F9F9` - A warm, slightly off-white that mimics high-quality book paper. It provides a softer reading experience than pure white. - **Ink (Primary Text):** `#1A1712` - A deep, warm charcoal. It has more character than black and complements the "Paper" background perfectly. ### Secondary & Muted Tones - **Parchment (Section Backgrounds):** `#F4F3F3` - Used for subtle tonal shifts in sections like the Footer or secondary content blocks. - **Dust (Muted Text/Labels):** `#7D766E` - A refined grey-brown used for secondary labels, dates, and less prominent UI elements in Inter Light. - **Vellum (Subtle Borders/Dividers):** `#CEC5BC` - Used very sparingly for thin, 1px rules when a hard separation is absolutely necessary. ### Accent (Used Sparingly) - **Obsidian (Primary Action):** `#2F2B26` - A slightly darker, more solid version of the ink color used for primary buttons or high-contrast call-to-actions. --- **Typography Reminder:** - **EB Garamond:** Headlines and Display - **Inter Light:** Navigation, Labels, and Body UI
